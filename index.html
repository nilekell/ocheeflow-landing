Principles

This is what
keeps us
in flow.

Six pillars. Nothing more. No shortcuts, no hacks — just the fundamentals you can learn in a day, but take a lifetime to master.

01

Nutrition

Whole foods. Not a diet — a default. What you put in determines what comes out. Eat to perform, recover, and sustain. Keep it simple, keep it real.

02

Consistency

Showing up when it's inconvenient is the work. Not the perfect session — the session that happens. Likkle by likkle, de bird build him nest.

03

Recovery

Rest is not weakness. Sleep, stillness, and repair are where adaptation happens. Recovery is part of the programme — not the absence of one.

04

Intention

Every session, every meal, every rest day — done with purpose. Intention turns routine into practice. It's the difference between going through the motions and building something.

05

Patience

The long game is the only game. Strength, endurance, and leanness are built over years — not weeks. Patient man ride donkey.

06

Reflection

Know what's working. Adjust what isn't. Reflection keeps you honest — it turns experience into progress rather than just repetition.
